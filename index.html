QuitLog Privacy Policy

At QuitLog, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our habit tracking and streak management app.

This policy is effective as of May 12, 2025.

Information We Collect

We collect information through third-party services to help us improve QuitLog and provide a better experience:

1. Amplitude Analytics

  • Device information (e.g., operating system, device model)
  • Log data (e.g., session duration, usage time)
  • Interaction data (e.g., which features are used)

2. Adapty SDK

  • In-app purchase history
  • Subscription status
  • Device identifiers, IP address, and region

QuitLog does not collect or store user-created habits or streak logs on our servers. All tracking data stays local unless explicitly synced through third-party services (e.g., analytics).

How We Use Your Information

  • Improve the app and add new features
  • Understand how users interact with the app
  • Manage in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Analyze user behavior to improve engagement
  • Ensure app performance and reliability
  • Provide personalized insights and suggestions

Tracking

We use tracking technologies through Amplitude and Adapty to understand app usage patterns and improve your experience. You can manage tracking preferences through your device settings.

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Payments are processed via the Apple App Store. We use Adapty SDK to manage access to premium features and analyze subscription behavior. We do not store any payment details on our servers.

Sharing Your Information

We may share limited data with the following partners strictly for functionality and analytics:

  • Amplitude (usage analytics)
  • Adapty (subscription and purchase handling)

We may also disclose information when required by law (e.g., court orders or legal processes).

How We Protect Your Information

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Your Choices

  • Limit tracking via your device privacy settings
  • Uninstall the app to stop all data collection
  • Use app settings (where available) to manage data usage

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated over time. Any significant changes will be communicated via app updates or in-app notifications.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at quitlogapp@gmail.com.
